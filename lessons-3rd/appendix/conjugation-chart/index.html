巻末 - Conjugation Chart

This chart shows all the verb conjugations learned in Genki volumes I and II. You can use this chart to review and compare conjugations you may be having trouble with. Additionally you can click the button below to start a quiz where you can practice using all the conjugations listed in the chart below.

Notes

  1. The forms with * are exceptions.
  2. The underlined portions show what is changed, with the exception of the negative ある forms where it is changed entirely into ない.
  3. The tilde represents the unchanged portion of the verb. (the part not underlined)
  4. "Genki I" and "Genki II" show what volume the conjugations were learned in.
