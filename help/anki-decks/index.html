Anki Decks for Genki

Here you can download Anki decks for Genki 2nd Edition and Genki 3rd Edition, based on the vocabulary found on Genki Study Resources and in the Genki textbooks. Click any of the links in the sections below to download a deck.


Quick Navigation

  • Genki Decks - 2nd Edition
  • Genki Decks - 3rd Edition
  • How to Import the Decks

If you would like to download all of the decks at once, click here to download the repository. Once downloaded, unzip the contents, then navigate to resources/tools/decks and open the folder for the 2nd Edition (lessons) or 3rd Edition (lessons-3rd) to access their decks.

You can also find xlsx vocabulary lists for Genki here!

How to Import the Decks

Once you have your decks, open Anki and click "Import File" (or go to File > Import...) to import your decks.

That's it, you're done. Now you're ready to study the vocab!
